
# VisitSecure-Visitra
This is a Flutter mobile app called Orientbell VMI (Visitor Management &amp; Inspection) built for Orientbell Tiles, a major Indian tile manufacturer.
The app manages who enters and exits their factory plants, with four user roles:
Visitor/Customer — registers for a visit, gets a digital badge with a QR code, and can view past visits.
Receptionist/Security Guard — scans QR codes at the gate to check visitors in/out, and manages the visitor list.
Host (Employee) — sees who's coming to meet them, can send directions to visitors.
Admin — gets a full dashboard with analytics, plant-wise footfall, and an AI-generated executive report.
The app also has a logistics module for truck drivers picking up tiles, where they can get AI recommendations on which vehicle suits their load weight.
It uses Firebase for authentication and real-time data, Google Gemini AI for features like scanning business cards and generating reports, and QR Flutter/Mobile Scanner for badge generation and scanning.
